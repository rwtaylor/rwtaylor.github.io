I study evolution in wild populations on a contemporary time scale. I am currently a postdoc in Dmitri Petrov's lab at Stanford University, where I am investigating fluctuating selection on Drosophila spp. in response to seasonal variaiton. I am also working on applying population genomics techniques to the Kluane Red Squirrel Project.

My PhD investigated the evolutionary maintenence of personality in a wild population of red squirrels. I was advised by Andrew McAdam at the University of Guelph and Kay Holekamp at Michigan State University.
